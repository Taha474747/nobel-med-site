Penlon Prima 320

The Penlon Prima 320 is a versatile anaesthesia system providing the ideal solution for today’s operating room. It features an 8.4” TFT colour touchscreen, up to six ventilation modes, and optional CO₂ and SpO₂ monitoring.

Penlon Prima 451 MRI

The Penlon Prima 451 is a compact MRI anaesthesia system, designed for use with 0.55, 1.5, 3, and 7 tesla scanners. It offers advanced patient support modes and features an electronic touchscreen ventilator.
